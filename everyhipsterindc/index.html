Every Bar in DC is a ridiculous 'DC' 'blog' about 'bars', but it's also about complaining about hipsters. Without going into the moronic, overplayed subject itself..., here's just the blog distilled.

if you want to go into the moronic, overplayed subject itself, the trick is to look for the inverse - who are the people who are not described as hipsters? everybarindc seems to think these are mainly either 'residents of the surrounding area', 'young professionals', people who like sports, and beer lovers - which neatly define hipsters in the inverse, though it's hard to invert 'beer lovers' when talking about people who are at a bar.
